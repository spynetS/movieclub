Turn your friend group into a movie club

Create clubs, share each member's movie lists, and take turns voting on randomized suggestions — the winning movie is what you watch next. Simple, democratic, and fun.

How it works

  1. Each member adds movies to their personal list.
  2. The club aggregates lists and randomly suggests candidates.
  3. Members vote — the top pick is the next movie night choice.

Why it’s fun

  • Discover movies your friends love.
  • Fair voting keeps things democratic.
  • Quick setup — ready in minutes.

Features that make movie nights easier

Everything you need to pick, vote, and enjoy the next watch together.

Shared movie lists

Each member keeps their own list — the club aggregates suggestions from everyone.

Randomized picks

We randomize candidates so the same people don't always pick — fairness + discovery.

Club voting

Vote on the candidates — simple up/down voting or score votes (configurable).
